Booking
How to reserve your Paris airport transfer

You can book your airport transfer in three ways: using the booking form on our website, calling our 24/7 hotline on +33 (0)1 43 327 081, or emailing us at contact@parisairportshuttle.fr.

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance, especially during peak travel seasons (summer, school holidays, public holidays). For airport minibus hire or corporate accounts, please allow 48–72 hours.

💡 Pro Tip
Booking early locks in your fixed rate. Prices never increase after booking — not even for delays or changes to your flight.

For standard airport transfers (Economy or VAN), we recommend booking at least 24 hours before your pickup. We do accept same-day bookings when availability allows — call us directly for last-minute requests.

For group bookings (VAN-XL or multiple vehicles), corporate accounts, and special occasion transfers, please book 48–72 hours in advance to guarantee the right vehicle and coordinator.

Pricing & Rates
Understanding our fixed transfer prices

Yes — 100% fixed. The price quoted at booking is the price you pay at drop-off. There are no meters running, no surge pricing, no airport add-ons, and no hidden charges.

  • Motorway tolls included
  • Airport access fees included
  • French VAT (TVA) included
  • Night transfers — same rate as daytime
  • Flight delays — no additional charge

Our fixed rates from Charles de Gaulle Airport to Paris city centre start from:

  • Economy (1–3 pax): from €65
  • VAN (1–6 pax): from €90
  • VAN-XL (up to 8 pax): from €110

All prices are per vehicle, not per person, and include all tolls, airport fees, and VAT. View the full rate table →

On Arrival
Finding your driver and flight tracking

Your driver will be waiting in the arrivals hall with a sign displaying your name (or the passenger's name if booked for a third party). This applies to CDG, Orly, and Beauvais airports.

Before your driver departs for the airport, you'll receive an SMS containing:

  • Driver's full name and photo
  • Vehicle make, colour, and plate number
  • Real-time driver location link
  • Direct driver contact number

Yes — flight tracking is included with every booking at no extra charge. We monitor your flight in real time and automatically adjust your driver's arrival time if your flight is delayed, diverted, or arrives early.

You don't need to call us to report a delay. Our system handles it automatically, so your driver is always there when you land — not before and not hours after.

Important
Please provide your accurate flight number at the time of booking so we can track the correct flight.

Your driver will wait up to 60 minutes free of charge after your flight lands. This gives you time to pass through immigration, collect luggage, and clear customs without rushing.

If you need more time due to unusual delays (long customs queues, lost luggage), simply call or text your driver directly — their number is sent in the pre-arrival SMS.

If your flight is cancelled, please contact us as soon as possible. As long as you notify us more than 24 hours before your originally scheduled pickup time, there is no cancellation charge.

We'll happily rebook your transfer for a new date and flight at no additional cost. Our team is reachable 24/7 at +33 (0)1 43 327 081.

If you can't locate your driver in the arrivals hall, don't worry — follow these steps:

  • Check the SMS for your driver's name and photo
  • Call the driver's direct number (included in your SMS)
  • If no answer, call our 24/7 support line: +33 (0)1 43 327 081

Our operations team can locate your driver in real time and reconnect you within minutes.

Cancellation & Changes
Our modification and cancellation policy
  • More than 24 hours before pickup: Free cancellation — full refund, no questions asked.
  • Within 24 hours of pickup: 50% cancellation fee applies.
  • No-show (driver waiting): Full fare charged.

To cancel, contact us by phone or email. Our 24/7 hotline is always available: +33 (0)1 43 327 081.

Yes — modifications are accepted free of charge up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup. Changes include: pickup time, destination address, passenger count, or vehicle type.

For changes within 24 hours of pickup, please call us directly so we can assess availability and advise on any applicable adjustment fee.

No extra charge for flight delays. We track your flight in real time and adjust the driver's arrival accordingly — at no cost to you.

If your flight is cancelled by the airline and you notify us as soon as possible, we'll rebook your transfer at no extra charge. If the delay is more than 24 hours and you can't travel at all, we treat it as a force-majeure situation and waive the cancellation fee — just contact us with your cancellation confirmation from the airline.

Contact us by email at contact@parisairportshuttle.fr or by phone with your booking reference number. Eligible refunds are processed within 5–7 business days back to your original payment method.

For queries about specific charges or disputed fees, our support team is available 24/7 to review your case.
